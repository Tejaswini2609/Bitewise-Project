About

BiteWise helps you pick the smarter food delivery deal

BiteWise brings Swiggy, Zomato and Magicpin into one comparison view. It checks price, discount, rating and delivery time, then recommends the platform that gives the best value for the food you searched.

One connected search

Typing in search or asking the AI assistant updates the same comparison cards.

Nearby-aware suggestions

Explore

Search, ask, compare and order

Use the search bar for direct food comparisons or ask the AI assistant naturally, such as "Find biryani under Rs. 250 near me". The comparison cards update with the assistant's matched food so both views stay connected.

Nearby first

When location access is enabled, BiteWise labels recommendations as nearby-aware and keeps platform links ready for local availability checks.

Fallback choices

If an exact food is not in the catalog, the app suggests similar foods and still points you to the best available platform search.

Assistant actions

Quick prompts help users compare cheapest, fastest and budget picks without typing the full question each time.
